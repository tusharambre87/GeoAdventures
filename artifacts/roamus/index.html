The gap nobody talks about

Plans are easy.
Execution is hard.

You did everything right. Google Maps, TripAdvisor, Reddit threads. But on the day — kids complaining, stops running long, parents buried in phones. The plan fell apart.

🗺️
Google Maps knows where to go.
It doesn't know you have a 7-year-old who needs a snack break, or that your family is done after stop 3. It gives you directions, not a day.
⭐
TripAdvisor knows what's popular.
It doesn't know what works for families like yours. A 4.8-star restaurant means nothing if no kid there is under 25.
📋
Trip planners plan beautifully.
But a great plan in Notes and a great day on the ground are different things. Nobody hands it to the family and says "run."
📱
RoamUs runs the day.
At each stop it knows what's next. It keeps your kids engaged. It captures the moment. You stay present. The app handles the rest.
How it works

Four layers.
One smooth day.

RoamUs covers the entire trip lifecycle — before, during, and after. Each layer builds on the last.

01 — BEFORE YOUR TRIP
🗓️
Build

Tell RoamUs where you're going, who's coming, and how you like to travel. It generates a smart itinerary — family-tested, not just tourist-popular.

Planning layer
02 — ON THE DAY
📍
Run

This is the whole product. At each stop, RoamUs shows exactly what to do, how long to stay, what to see first. No decisions. No searching. Just be there.

Core experience
03 — FOR YOUR KIDS
