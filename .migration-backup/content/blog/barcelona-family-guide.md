## Is Barcelona Good for Families?

Barcelona is an outstanding family destination when approached correctly. Our Kid-Fit Index scores Barcelona **91/100 for ages 5–8** and **88/100 for ages 9–12**. Families with toddlers (under 5) score it lower at 72/100 — it's manageable but requires more planning.

Barcelona's metro is stroller-friendly on most lines. Taxis are cheap and easy. Heat above 30°C is common June–August — schedule indoor stops at midday.

## What to Skip (Honest)

These are the stops that look good on travel blogs but consistently underperform for families.

### Las Ramblas
Overcrowded, pickpocket hotspot, nothing specifically good for kids. Skip and walk one street back.

### Camp Nou Tour
Only works if your kids are actual football fans. Otherwise it's a 90-minute walk through empty stands.
