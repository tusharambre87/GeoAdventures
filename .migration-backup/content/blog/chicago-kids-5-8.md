## Is Chicago Good for Kids Aged 5–8?

Chicago is an excellent destination for this age group, with 93 out of 100 on the GeoAdventures Kid-Fit Index. The city has a strong mix of hands-on, visual, and outdoor experiences that land well with ages 5–8 (early school age).

Chicago's L train is easy to navigate. Uber/Lyft are affordable. The lakefront is flat and very stroller-friendly. Michigan Avenue is walkable between most major attractions.

---

## Top 5 Stops for Ages 5–8 (Early school age)

### 1. Field Museum

**Kid-Fit Score: 90/100** | Best age: 5+ | Best time: Morning | Plan for: 2 hrs

Sue the T-Rex is on the main floor — you'll see her the moment you walk in.

![Family exploring the Field Museum, Chicago](/images/blog/chicago-kids-5-8/field-museum.jpg)

### 2. Shedd Aquarium

**Kid-Fit Score: 95/100** | Best age: 3+ | Best time: Morning | Plan for: 2 hrs

Book online — walk-up queues on weekends are long. Beluga show books out fast.

![Kids at Shedd Aquarium watching the marine exhibits](/images/blog/chicago-kids-5-8/shedd-aquarium.jpg)

### 3. Navy Pier

**Kid-Fit Score: 92/100** | Best age: 4+ | Best time: Afternoon | Plan for: 90 min

The Ferris wheel has great views. The Children's Museum inside is worth an hour.

![Family at Navy Pier with the Ferris wheel in the background](/images/blog/chicago-kids-5-8/navy-pier.jpg)

### 4. Millennium Park

**Kid-Fit Score: 85/100** | Best age: All ages | Best time: Any | Plan for: 60 min

The Crown Fountain splash pad is free and kids love it in summer.

### 5. Lincoln Park Zoo

**Kid-Fit Score: 93/100** | Best age: 3+ | Best time: Morning | Plan for: 2 hrs

Free admission. One of the best free zoos in the US. Go early.

![Children at Lincoln Park Zoo, one of the best free zoos in the US](/images/blog/chicago-kids-5-8/lincoln-park-zoo.jpg)

## What to Skip (Honest)

**Willis Tower Skydeck** — Fun for a minute but expensive for families. The Bean at Millennium Park is free and equally memorable.

**Chicago Architecture Boat Tour** — Great for adults, genuinely boring for most kids under 10. Save it for a grown-up trip.
